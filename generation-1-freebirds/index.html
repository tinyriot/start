Generation 1: Immutable Freebirds

By Tiny Riot
April 16, 2022

First ever TinyRiot NFT generation was created to support Ukrainian people (in future there will be another humanitarian problems) and to start talking about free of speech that was suppressed last years and especially during last month. 

All collections in this generation will use built-in royalty concept to gather funds for humanitarian needs - just buy or sell the token for any price and you are participant.

This Generation was named "Immutable Freebirds" because everyone who is standing for humanity, peace and free of speech can became immutable and free. I am standing on theirs side.

This projects works can be splitted into:

  1. Generations: - includes all works that share the same base technology or idea. 
    Example:
    GEN1: "Immutable FreeBirds" NFTs with built-in royalties* that are used to gather funds on safe and creditable crypto-address for it further transfer to humanitarian organizations like Red Cross. 
    GEN2 (planned to start after GEN1 Collection is minted out): "Dynamic CryptoBirds".... 
    1. Iterations = Collections: - variations of idea or technological implementation. Iterations can be used to track the evolution of an idea/and its technical implementation. Prices for art-tech works may vary depending on amount of time and other resources that was spent for collection creation.
      Examples:
      a. Handpicked "Modern FreeBirds" - digital bird art generated with tuned OpenAI help by combinating different weights and parameters.
      b. "Songs of chirping FreeBirds" - digital bird art generated with extra trained OpenAI based on citations from certain songs that inspired me during this project. 
      c. "Crypto-dream wisebirds" - digital moving bird art generated in honor of extraordinary people citations for peace made using multiple AI algorithms. 

This generation will include 3x collections/iterations (all 3 collections will be publish when current works will be sold - its creation is not so cheap, especially during high gas prices):

  1. Handpicked "Modern FreeBirds" - 520 digital bird art objects generated with tuned OpenAI help by combinating different weights and parameters. "Modern FreeBirds" are free to be themselves, be not afraid to speak and live in peace. There are only 520 FreeBirds in the world for now.
  2. "Songs of chirping FreeBirds" - 50 digital bird art were generated with extra trained OpenAI based on citations from certain songs that inspired me during this project. Some freebirds are chirping inspiring songs about peace and freedom. These are songs for peace and freedom that I was listening while working on this project. Every Chirping FreeBird NFT token will be added in 3x exemplars, so you can form social groups with other owners of this certain song-based art.
  3. "Crypto-dream wisebirds" - 11 digital moving bird art generated in honor of extraordinary people citations (Albert Einstein, Martin Luther King Jr. ...) for peace made using multiple AI algorithms. This collection covers some of the wisest and most inpirational phrases of famous FreeBirds from the past. Collection includes 11 2.5D moving images. One of Images will be created specially for TinyRiot Royalty address governor. 

Special collection may be added in colloboration with some marvelous people.
