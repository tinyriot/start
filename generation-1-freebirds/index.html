Generation 1: Immutable Freebirds

By Tiny Riot
April 16, 2022

First ever TinyRiot NFT generation was created to support Ukrainian people (in future there will be another humanitarian problems) and to start talking about free of speech that was suppress last years and especially during last month. 

All collections in this generation will use built-in royalty concept to gather funds for humanitarian needs - just buy or sell the token for any price and you are participant.

This Generation was named "Immutable Freebirds" because everyone who is standing for humanity, peace and free of speech can became immutable and free. I am standing on theirs side.

This generation will include 3x collections/iterations:

  1. Handpicked "Modern FreeBirds" - digital generative art generated with tuned OpenAI help by combinating different weights and parameters. "Modern FreeBirds" are free to be themselves, be not afraid to speak and live in peace. There are only 520 FreeBirds in the world for now.
  2. "Songs of chirping FreeBirds" - 50 digital bird art were generated with extra trained OpenAI based on citations from certain songs that inspired me during this project. Some freebirds are chirping inspiring songs about peace and freedom. These are songs for peace and freedom that I was listening while working on this project.
  3. "Crypto-dream wisebirds" - 11 digital moving bird art generated in honor of extraordinary people citations (Albert Einstein, Martin Luther King Jr. ...) for peace made using multiple AI algorithms. This collection covers some of the wisest and most inpirational phrases of famous FreeBirds from the past. Collection includes 11 2.5D moving images. One of Images will be created specially for TinyRiot Royalty address governor. 

Special collection may be added in colloboration with some marvelous people.
