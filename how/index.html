HOW / Royalty concept

By Tiny Riot
April 18, 2022

TinyRiot project will use a wide range of modern technologies and concepts and will not stick to one blockchain or AI algorithm, etc. - it will evolutionary constantly. Example: NFTs will be minted not only on Ethereum but also using Solana, possibly Next or Doge might be another blockchain. Target is only one - maximize positive social impact.

This first generation is a Proof-of-Concept for NFT Royalties* used as an automatic donation mechanism that gathers royalties from all sell/buy NFT actions to one creditable crypto address that will forward these funds to non-military, non-governmental humanitarian organizations that helps local civilians during humanitarian catastrophes...

The ultimate goal for Generation X will be balanced DAO development that also uses NFTs as voting tokens (some of them should be launched before DAO creation, it might be one or multiple NFT Generation mix).

All NFTs will have the same Royalty crypto-address. Royalties will be managed using Studio and Royalty Register made by manifold.xyz - Example: https://royaltyregistry.xyz/0x4857fa75255fbad80023fa57b51995b73f35b43b/35 

Royalty crypto-address that will be managed by governor. Amount of royalties may vary between several series, but must stay in interval from 10 and up to 25%. For 99% of NFTs from first generation royalty will be set to 22% (in case of OpenSea it can be less - 10%).

Overall NFT royalty scheme 

* Royalties

Some NFTs will automatically pay out royalties to their creators when they're sold. This is still a developing concept but it's one of the most powerful. Original owners of EulerBeats Originals earn an 8% royalty every time the NFT is sold on. And some platforms, like Foundation and Zora, support royalties for their artists. This is completely automatic so creators can just sit back and earn royalties as their work is sold from person to person. At the moment, figuring out royalties is very manual and lacks accuracy – a lot of creators don't get paid what they deserve. If your NFT has a royalty programmed into it, you'll never miss out.

Source: https://ethereum.org/en/nft/#royalties

In futher generations amount of royalties can be tuned to reach maximum positive social impact so it may vary between several series, but never will be set to less then 10%.
