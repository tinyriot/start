WHAT

By Tiny Riot
April 2, 2022

TinyRiot is a project focused on positive social impact started with the idea of WEB3, AI, and crypto technology exploration and pursuing opportunities for its' practical implementation to resolve some of the global problems. 

And the first step is NFTs series with royalties transfered to humanitarian needs.

This project is based on four beliefs:

  1. Our planet, our world is a very complicated system where we all, each man and woman are a small part of it connected to each other and working together in cooperation even if we do not see or feel that in everyday life. Last decades our society and economics grew thanks to this cooperation and interconnections. We are interconnected economically, technologically, and mentally, we all are one organism and should live in peace and care each other, not destroying this connection between nations us.
  2. We were born to evolutionate and develop, research this world. That means we should become better every day and try to give more than we receive. If you do something, help this world and try to do your work better tomorrow than it was done yesterday.
  3. Our existing systems are not stable now and our society has changed for the last 75 years more than it changed during the previous 2000 years. The next step in our world development should be a decentralized and self-balanced system made in the right way. What it should be - research should be done.
  4. If you are learning or creating something then try to do it with a less serious mindset. The biggest mistakes were made with the most serious faces. 

So I will try to make the world a little better and more united by mixing crypto, AI, psychology and art together.
