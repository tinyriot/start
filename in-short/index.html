Project

You are on the project's webpage - https://tinyriot.xyz. 
The major communication channel for this account is The New Twitter (@TinyRiotReal).

This project was started with a thought in my head to develop the concept of NFTs Royalty, use it for some more usefull, bigger things and that are avaible right now. NFT Royalty will be used to accumulate funds on 1 (or several) humanitarian crypto-addresses that would be managed by 1 or several governors for the first time.

When the governor will provide ETH new crypto-address that he will make by himself, then from my part - I will update in the TinyRiot contracts Royalty crypto-address field with the new one, received from crypto-address governor. That actions mean that a gentlemen's agreement between Us and the Governor is concluded .

This(-ese) humanitarian crypto-address(-es) will be used only for 1 (one) purpose - to distribute accumulated funds between proven humanitarian non-military, non-government organizations (example: RedCross) that are working locally where humanitarian catastrophe acceded. If there will not be any catastrophes - no transfers will be made at that time. During that period crypto-addresses (Set via Manifold Contracts) will stay in the accumulation regime.

Payments should be done at least once a year (preferably once a month) and all fund transfers from this account must be publicly announced with all details provided.

While I will be developing humanitarian part of the concept is planned to create multiple NFT collections using different technologies. The works from Gen 2 will start Dynamic NFTs - let's invent new ways of doing good things by mixing old and new concepts.

Also during the first year is planned to add a basic DAO mechanism and start to develop a fully automatic DAO mechanism.

P.S. Average Humanitarian NFT Royalty amount for works of this project will be no less than 22.1% and plus-minus is equal to the average VAT in European Union. Plus as a symbol for myself - be because I am trying to solve multiple Catch 22 (Wiki) situations. 

Humanitarian Royalty will be sent to Governor provided crypto-account(-s) (Set via Manifold Contract).
