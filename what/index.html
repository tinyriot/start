WHAT

By Tiny Riot
April 18, 2022

TinyRiot is a project focused on positive social impact started with the idea of WEB3, AI, and crypto technology exploration and pursuing opportunities for its' practical implementation to resolve some of the global problems. 

The first problem aimed to be solved - humanitarian fund credibility and its availability to gather needed funding in time. To solve that problem was decided to use a concept of NFTs with built-in royalties.

This project is based on four beliefs:

  1. Our planet, our world is a very complicated system where we all are interconnected economically, technologically, and mentally, we all are one organism and should live in peace and care each other, not destroying this connection between nations us. Working on this project I hope to recreate some of them and build new ones.
  2. We were born to evolutionate and develop, research this world. And this project will evalutionate too, might be even with your participation...
  3. Our society has changed more in last 75 years than in the previous 2000 years, but the concepts we still use are the same as before.. The next step in mankind development should be a decentralized and self-balanced system made in the right way.  And to figure out what the right way should look like is a one of this project aims.
  4. If you are learning or creating something then try to do it with a less serious mindset. The biggest mistakes were made with the most serious faces. 

So with your help will try to make the world a little better place to live and more united by mixing crypto, AI, socialogy and art into one recipe.
