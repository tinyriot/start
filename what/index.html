WHAT

By Tiny Riot
April 18, 2022

TinyRiot is a project focused on positive social impact started with the idea of WEB3, AI, and crypto technology exploration and pursuing opportunities for its' practical implementation to resolve some of the global problems. 

The first problem aimed to be solved - humanitarian fund credibility and its availability to gather needed funding in time. To solve that problem was decided to use a concept of NFTs with built-in royalties.

This project is based on four beliefs:

  1. Our planet, our world is a very complicated system where we all are interconnected economically, technologically, and mentally, we all are one organism and should live in peace and care each other, not destroying this connection between us. While working on this project I hope to recreate some of them and build new ones.
  2. We were born to evolutionate and develop, research this world. And this project will evalutionate too, might be even with your participation...
  3. Our society has changed more in last 75 years than in the previous 2000 years, but the concepts we still use are the same as before. The next step for the mankind development should be further integration of decentralized and self-balanced systems made in the right way.  To figure out what the right way should look like is a one of this project major aims.
  4. If you are learning or creating something then try to do it with a less serious mindset. The biggest mistakes were made with the most serious faces. 

So with your help, together we can try to make the world a little better place to live and more united while creating some cool stuff mixing crypto, AI, socialogy and art into one recipe.
