WHAT

By Tiny Riot
April 15, 2022

TinyRiot is a project focused on positive social impact started with the idea of WEB3, AI, and crypto technology exploration and pursuing opportunities for its' practical implementation to resolve some of the global problems. 

This project is a responce for unstability in this world. As the first step will launch NFTs series with royalties being used for humanitarian needs.

This project is based on four beliefs:

  1. Our planet, our world is a very complicated system where we all are interconnected economically, technologically, and mentally, we all are one organism and should live in peace and care each other, not destroying this connection between nations us. Working on this project I will try to grow and heal this connections between us.
  2. We were born to evolutionate and develop, research this world. And this project will evalutionate too, might be even with your help!
  3. Our existing economical systems are not stable now and our society has changed for the last 75 years more than it changed during the previous 2000 years. The next step in humanity development should be a decentralized and self-balanced system made in the right way.  And what the right way should look like - research will be done, this is one of targets during project development.
  4. If you are learning or creating something then try to do it with a less serious mindset. The biggest mistakes were made with the most serious faces. 

So with your help will try to make the world a little better place to live and more united by mixing crypto, AI, psychology and art into one recipe.
