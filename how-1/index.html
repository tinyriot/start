HOW

By Tiny Riot
April 15, 2022

TinyRiot project will use a wide range of modern technologies and concepts and will not stick to one blockchain or AI algorithm, etc. - it will evolutionary constantly. Example: NFTs will be minted not only on Ethereum but also using Solana, Next, or might be another blockchain. Target is only one - maximize positive social impact.

This projects works can split into:

  1. Generations: - includes all works that share the same base technology or idea. 
    Example:
    GEN1: "Immutable Birds" NFTs with built-in royalties* that are used to gather funds on safe and creditable crypto-address for it further transfer to humanitarian organizations like Red Cross. 
    GEN2 (planned to start after GEN1 Collection is minted out): "Dynamic CryptoBirds".... 
    1. Iterations = Collections: - variations of idea or technological implementation. Iterations can be used to track the evolution of an idea/and its technical implementation. Prices for art-tech works may vary depending on amount of time and other resources that was spent for collection creation.
      Examples:
      a. Handpicked "Immutable Birds" - digital bird art generated with tuned OpenAI help by combinating different weights and parameters.
      b. "Immutable SongBirds" - digital bird art generated with extra trained OpenAI based on citations from certain songs that inspired me during this project. 
      c. "Immutable Dreambirds" - digital moving bird art generated in honor of extraordinary people citations for peace made using multiple AI algorithms. 

So in essence this first generation is a Proof-of-Concept for NFT Royalties* used as an automatic donation mechanism that gathers royalties from all sell/buy actions to one creditable crypto address that will forward these funds to non-military, non-governmental humanitarian organizations that helps locally civilians during humanitarian catastrophes... The target for Gen X will be DAO balancer development that also uses NFTs as voting tokens.

Overall NFT royalty scheme 

 

* Royalties

Some NFTs will automatically pay out royalties to their creators when they're sold. This is still a developing concept but it's one of the most powerful. Original owners of EulerBeats Originals earn an 8% royalty every time the NFT is sold on. And some platforms, like Foundation and Zora, support royalties for their artists. This is completely automatic so creators can just sit back and earn royalties as their work is sold from person to person. At the moment, figuring out royalties is very manual and lacks accuracy – a lot of creators don't get paid what they deserve. If your NFT has a royalty programmed into it, you'll never miss out.

Source: https://ethereum.org/en/nft/#royalties

Donation royalty amount will be tuned to reach maximum positive social impact but it never will be set to less then 15%.
